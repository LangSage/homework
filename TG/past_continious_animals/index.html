Past Continuous

Positive: I was walking. / They were playing.

Negative: The dog wasn't barking. / We weren't sleeping.

Question: Was she feeding the cat? / Were they looking for the puppy?

Используем для действия, которое происходило в конкретный момент в прошлом: at 7 o'clock yesterday, last night, at that moment.
